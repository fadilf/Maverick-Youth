About Maverick Youth



“Older men declare war. But it is youth that must fight and die” – Herbert Hoover.

A phrase, not uncommon in present day era, is ‘Youth Empowerment’. It is a phrase that is popular with the media, a commonly used rhetoric by politicians, and an overused statement in school seminars. That being said, the opportunities present for the youth to express their opinions openly on issues that concern them are minimal, if not non-existent.

This is where Maverick Youth comes into play. This is a platform where any high school or university student can post their views on global and regional issues, thought provoking ideas or topics that are of great concern to them. It gives them a powerful literary voice. A voice that can truly enable them to change the world for the better and do full justice to the phrase ‘Youth Empowerment’.

Why read or contribute to this site?
Posting or reading content on our website has several advantages:
1. It’s absolutely free.
2. Improve your literary skills. These skills are valuable at every stage of life.
3. You get to express your own opinions. Your views don’t have to conform to public or media opinion. If your article has logically backed viewpoints, we will be more than happy to post them.

So what are you waiting for! Register and start posting your articles. Note, posted articles need to be approved before opening them up to public viewing. The only cases where we won’t publish content is when there is profanity, extremism, insufficient content or plagiarism present in your article. Articles should be typically between 300-1200 words.
